Just Some Cookie Recipe

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Mix together 1 cup of butter, 1 cup of white sugar, and 1 cup of packed brown sugar until smooth. Beat in 2 eggs, one at a time, then stir in 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. Dissolve a teaspoon of baking soda in 2 teaspoons of hot water. Add to batter along with 1/2 a teaspoon of salt. Stir in 3 cups of flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
